What's New in v1.0.4
----------------------
Old Toontown Central has been given a graphical improvement.
Turrets have been optimized to not cause as much lag.
There's a limit of 3 Turrets per battle area to ensure balance.
Coach's Battle Shop now shows the number of turrets purchased.
Coach's Battle Shop now shows the correct amount underneath the turret(s) you have.
The VP's flashing has been returned and fixed to not cause client crashes.
Uno has been enabled again, please report any bugs.
Fixed glitchy animations with gags.
Fixed animations in the tutorial.
