Cog Invasion Online 0.9.3.5 Release Notes
---------------------------------------
- You can now view the total game population on the Places page in your Shticker Book.
- You can also view the Record Population on the Places page. This is the highest population that the game has ever had.
- Increased accuracy of bullets in Toon Battle.
- The game will close on you if you try to use speed hacks.
